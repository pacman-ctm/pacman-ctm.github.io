Phạm Anh Cường

MSc student in Machine Learning @ MBZUAI

Hi, I am Cuong Pham (my full name in Vietnamese is Phạm Anh Cường). I am a first-year MSc student in Machine Learning at MBZUAI, and my initial supervisor is Professor Samuel Horváth. My interests are Mathematics for AI and Applications of AI.

Before joining MBZUAI, I got my honors BSc in IT from VNU-UET, Vietnam in 2023. During my undergraduate, I was a member of DSKT Laboratory, supervised by Assoc. Prof. Quang-Thuy Ha and Dr. Van-Quang Nguyen. In industry, I used to work at GHTK (as an AI Engineer) and then at Viettel Networks (as a Data Scientist).
