**Phạm Anh Cường** (Anh-Cuong Pham, Cuong Pham) is a fresh graduate in Information Technology (honors program) from [VNU University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam](https://uet.vnu.edu.vn/en/) with a High Distinction degree from June 2023. Now, he works as a Data Scientist at [Viettel Networks](), one of the largest telecommunication companies in Vietnam. Before working as a Data Scientist, he was a Student Research in Data Science and Knowledge Technology Laboratory, doing researched in Deep Learning tasks. His bachelor's thesis was about Image Captioning in Vietnamest context, under the supervision of [Assoc. Prof. Quang-Thuy Ha](https://uet.vnu.edu.vn/~thuyhq/). Back to high school, he used to a specialized student in Mathematics, and participated in the 2019 Vietnam Mathematics Olympiad.

With strong background in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and true interest in Mathematics, his research interests focus on **Mathematics for AI** and **Applications of AI**.

He is also a recipient of the [2024 POSCO Asia Fellowship](https://www.postf.org/en/asia/purpose?ckattempt=1) for Master's study in Korean schools (focus on STEM majors) starting in 2024 Fall, and he is still seeking a Master's opportunity to activate the fellowship.
