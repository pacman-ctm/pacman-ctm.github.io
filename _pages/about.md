Hi, I am **Cuong Pham** (my full name in Vietnamese is **Phạm Anh Cường**). I am a first-year MSc student in Machine Learning at [MBZUAI](https://mbzuai.ac.ae/), and my initial supervisor is [Professor Samuel Horváth](https://sites.google.com/view/samuelhorvath). My interests are **Mathematics for AI** and **Applications of AI**.

Before joining MBZUAI, I got my honors BSc in IT from [VNU-UET, Vietnam](https://uet.vnu.edu.vn/en/) in 2023. During my undergraduate, I was a member of [DSKT Laboratory](https://www.fit.uet.vnu.edu.vn/en/knowledge-technology-and-data-science-laboratory/), supervised by [Assoc. Prof. Quang-Thuy Ha](https://uet.vnu.edu.vn/~thuyhq/) and [Dr. Van-Quang Nguyen](https://scholar.google.de/citations?user=qR180McAAAAJ&hl=en). In industry, I used to work at [GHTK](https://www.linkedin.com/company/giaohangtietkiem-vn/) (as an AI Engineer) and then at [Viettel Networks](https://www.linkedin.com/company/viettel-network/) (as a Data Scientist).
