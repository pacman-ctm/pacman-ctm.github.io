Phạm Anh Cường

BSc. in IT (honors) from UET-VNU, Data Scientist @ Viettel Networks

Cau Giay, Hanoi, Vietnam

Phạm Anh Cường (Anh-Cuong Pham, Cuong Pham) is a fresh graduate in Information Technology (honors program) from VNU University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam with a High Distinction bachelor’s degree from June 2023. Now, he is a Data Scientist at Viettel Networks, one of the largest telecommunication companies in Vietnam. Before working as a Data Scientist, he was a Student Research in Deep Learning at DSKT Laboratory, UET-VNU. His bachelor’s thesis was about Image Captioning in Vietnamese context, under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Quang-Thuy Ha. Back to high school, he used to be a specialized student in Mathematics, and participated in the 2019 Vietnam Mathematics Olympiad.

With strong background in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and true interest in Mathematics, his interests focus on Mathematics for AI and Applications of AI.

He is also a recipient of the 2024 POSCO Asia Fellowship for Master’s study in Korean schools (focus on STEM majors) starting in 2024 Fall semester, because of that, he is still seeking a Master’s opportunity to activate the fellowship.
